Pizza

Description

Pizza is perhaps the most famous dish in the entire world. It is loved by billions of people each year. Originating in Italy as far back as 997 AD pizza is a limitless staple of the human diet.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce

  • Mozzarella Cheese

  • Pepperoni

  • Basil

  • Bell Peppers

  • Red Onions

  1. Choose your crust. Perferably something homemade!
  2. Spread with sauce. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce
  3. Layer cheese and veggies. Sprinkle a thin layer of mozzarella cheese, leafy greens, vegiatables, and then sprinkle a little more mozzaarella and parmesan cheese over the top.
  4. Bake. for 10 minutes, or until the cheese is nice and gooey, remove from the over and garnish sparingly with fresh basil and quality olive oil if you desire
